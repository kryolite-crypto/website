How-to mine?
  1. Download wallet for you platform
  2. Launch wallet and go to "Addresses" tab
  3. Create new address by clicking on "New Address"
  4. Go to mining tab and click "Start mining"
  5. (Optional) Join our Discord
How-to stake?
  1. Open wallet and go to "Validator" tab
  2. Click on the address to copy it
  3. Go to "Send" tab
  4. Select wallet where to send from and paste copied address to "Recipient address"
  5. Set amount to send to your staking address (minimum stake is 20 000 kryo)
  6. Go to "Validator" tab again and wait for transaction to complete
  7. Click on "Enable Validator" and set address for stake rewards. This can be same as your staking address to automatically re-stake your rewards
  8. Your staking address will show up in explorer after transaction completes
