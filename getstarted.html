How-to mine in 5 steps?

  1. Download wallet and miner for your platform
  2. Launch wallet
  3. Go to addresses tab
  4. Right click on pre-generated address to copy the address
  5. Keep wallet running and launch miner with command 
    kryolite-miner --address kryo:5VWDAcZPXZRPJzBncuUCyeCVgJoEoDz7tWeB
WARNING This is an experimental project. There will be issues and testnet will be rolled back as needed. API should be considered unstable and subject to change.
